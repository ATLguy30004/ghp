## BRAND VOICE GUIDE

### What Hayaan Sounds Like (Authentic Teen Builder)

**Characteristics:**
- Short, punchy sentences
- Casual but not sloppy
- Specific, not vague
- Honest about what you don't know
- Zero buzzwords
- Shows work, doesn't just claim results

**Example Good Tweets:**
- "Built a landing page for my family's food trailer. Sold out 2 events. Turns out good copy > fancy animations."
- "Taught a kid binary today using Legos. He explained it back to me better than I explained it to him."
- "Spent 3 hours debugging CSS. The problem was a typo. I'm a professional."

**Example Bad Tweets (LinkedIn Kid Vibes):**

- "5 lessons I learned from scaling my family's food business"
- "The future of education is here, and I'm helping build it"

**Why the difference?**
- Good: Shows, doesn't tell. Specific. Sounds like a real person.
- Bad: Generic. Performative. Sounds like a 45-year-old growth hacker cosplaying as a teen.

**Too Casual (sounds immature):**
- "lol just coded a whole website while watching netflix"
- "school is so boring when will i ever use this"

**Too Professional (sounds fake):**
- "Leveraging my technical expertise to drive digital transformation"
- "Passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems"
- "Seeking opportunities to collaborate with like-minded innovators"

**Just Right (sounds authentic):**
- "Built a thing. It worked. Here's what I learned."
- "Turns out [specific insight]. Didn't expect that."
- "Spent the weekend [specific project]. [Specific result]."

- "Just hit 100 followers! So grateful!"
- "Honored to announce [normal thing]"

- "Huge announcement coming soon!"
- "Working on something that will change everything"

**Why it backfires:** If you have something to share, share it. If you don't, don't tease it. Teasing without delivery kills credibility.

- "Tag someone who needs to see this"
- "Drop a emoji if you agree"
- "RT if you think [obvious thing]"

**Why it backfires:** Desperation. Focus on quality content, not chasing likes.

## AUTHENTICITY CHECKLIST

Before you post anything, run it through this filter:

1. **Is this specific?** (YES = good, NO = too vague)
2. **Would I say this out loud to a real person?** (YES = authentic, NO = performative)
3. **Am I showing work or just making a claim?** (Showing = good, Claiming = bad)
4. **Does this teach something or share real progress?** (YES = valuable, NO = noise)
5. **Would this sound cringe if someone read it out loud in 5 years?** (Cringe = don't post)

**If 4-5 YES answers:** Post it.
**If 2-3 YES answers:** Rewrite it.
**If 0-1 YES answers:** Delete it.
